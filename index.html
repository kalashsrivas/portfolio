About Us

Welcome to GL Bajaj Institute of Management, Greater Noida Driven by a strong inclination towards imparting quality education, GL Bajaj Institute of Management, Greater Noida is one of the finest Educational Institute in the Greater Noida/Delhi-NCR region. Since the establishment of GL Bajaj Group, our skilled faculty share their knowledge with their students in the best possible way to find solutions to their complex issues and problems. Since its establishment in the year 1997, GL Bajaj Group has embraced quality and innovation favourable for the overall development of its students. Continuing on its path of growth, GL Bajaj Group experienced the greatest period of success in its history with 12 campuses located in NCR and Mathura. GL Bajaj Group has been successfully building strong professionals in the field of Management, Technology, Education, Dental Science, Engineering, Pharmacy and Library Sciences. Thanks to Mr. Pankaj Agrawal, Vice Chairman, GL Bajaj Educational Institutions, whose selfless work has won laurels for the GL Bajaj Group at various platforms. GL Bajaj Institute of Management, Greater Noida is one of the prestigious Institutions under the GL Bajaj Group offering Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) and Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA). The Institute is affiliated to Chaudhary Charan Singh University (CCS), Meerut.
